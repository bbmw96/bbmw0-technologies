About BBMW0 Technologies

BBMW0 Technologies is a UK-based software company built by engineers who believe precision tools should be accessible to professionals everywhere.

We believe software should do the hard work, so professionals can focus on the work that matters. Every product we build starts with the same question: what does a professional truly need? The answer is always the same: something powerful, reliable, and built to last.

Based in London, United Kingdom, with connections across Malaysia, Singapore, and Japan. Building a portfolio of tools for professionals worldwide.
